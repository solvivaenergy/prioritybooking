1. Purpose of the Booking Fee

The Booking Fee is a purely voluntary arrangement whereby the Client pays the amount stipulated in Section 2 for the sole purpose of securing a priority site assessment schedule with Solviva. The Booking Fee shall be subject to these Terms and Conditions.

2. Amount

The Client shall, upon execution of the Terms, pay to Solviva the sum of Five Thousand Pesos (₱5,000.00) as the Booking Fee. This Booking Fee shall be inclusive of Value Added Tax (VAT), withholding tax and all other applicable taxes.

3. Application of the Booking Fee

3.1 The Booking Fee shall be credited toward the final Contract Price should the nominated Project Site be found eligible for a rooftop solar PV system, and the Client proceeds with the execution of the Project Documents (as defined below) with Solviva.

3.2 Solviva in its sole discretion may opt to refund the Booking Fee to the Client for any reason whatsoever including for reasons specified in Section 6.1 of the Terms.

3.3 Upon confirmation of payment of the Booking Fee, Solviva will: tag the account internally as a Priority Booking account; and prioritize the Client for site assessment scheduling, subject to serviceability, access, and qualification checks.

4. Site Assessment Scheduling

4.1 Solviva shall use commercially reasonable efforts to schedule the Client’s site assessment within five (5) Business Days from payment confirmation. This timeline is a target only and may be affected by factors outside of Solviva’s control, including but not limited to:

  • Client availability;
  • Site accessibility;
  • Weather conditions;
  • Safety considerations;
  • Installer or assessor availability;
  • Coverage limitations;
  • Other analogous factors.

5.1 The Client acknowledges that the payment of the Booking Fee does not guarantee:

  • Project feasibility, project acceptance, or execution of the appropriate Solviva Solar Photovoltaic System Contract or equivalent document (“Project Documents”) between the Client and Solviva;

5.2 The Client must satisfy Solviva’s applicable qualification, technical, and serviceability requirements. Nothing in this Terms shall be construed as a commitment or obligation on the part of Solviva to execute the Project Documents with the Client.

6. Non-Refundability

6.1 The Booking Fee is non-refundable once paid, except in the following cases:

  • Solviva is unable to conduct the site assessment on the Agreed Schedule through no fault of the Client and the Client does not wish to proceed with the site assessment on a different date.

Provided that, where practicable, Solviva shall have the option to either (i) offer to the Client reasonable service recovery or accommodation, or (ii) refund the Booking Fee to the Client, without interest.
